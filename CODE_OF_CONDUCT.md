## ⚖️ CODE OF CONDUCT

✨ **WELCOME TO THE AUTODOC.AI COMMUNITY!**

### 📜 Our Pledge
We are dedicated to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors. As members and contributors of AutoDoc.ai, we pledge to make participation a harassment-free and enriching experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation. 

Because our project intersects with Artificial Intelligence, we also commit to principles of Responsible AI, ensuring our tools are built and used ethically.

### 🤝 Our Community Standards

✅ **What We Encourage 👇**
* **Showing Empathy & Respect:** Toward fellow contributors and users of the platform.
* **Using Welcoming Language:** Creating an inclusive space for developers of all skill levels.
* **Constructive Feedback:** Helping each other grow technically through thorough, kind code reviews.
* **Accepting Responsibility:** Owning mistakes, whether it's a broken build or a miscommunication, and learning from them gracefully.
* **Responsible Innovation:** Ensuring AI prompts and backend logic do not encourage or generate harmful, biased, or malicious code documentation.

❌ **What We Discourage (Zero Tolerance)**
* Harassment, exclusionary jokes, or discrimination of any kind.
* Trolling, disparaging comments, or derogatory remarks regarding someone's code or experience level.
* Submitting PRs that intentionally inject malicious prompts or bypass AI safety guardrails.
* Any act that makes others uncomfortable or unsafe.

### 🛡️ Our Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior to ensure a safe and productive environment. We commit to taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior.

Maintainers pledge to lead by example—using inclusive language, providing timely and respectful feedback, and prioritizing the project’s open-source mission over personal status. We will not tolerate the abuse of authority, silencing of valid technical dissent, or favoritism. 

### 💡 Key Elements Included:
* **Active Duty:** Monitoring the repository, PRs, and community discussions.
* **Ethical Boundary:** Upholding Responsible AI practices and data privacy.
* **Leadership Role:** Setting a respectful tone for open-source collaboration.

### ❤️ Our Commitment
We are dedicated to fostering a culture of mutual respect and continuous learning. By contributing to this project, we pledge to uphold these values and hold ourselves accountable to the community.

Thank you for making AutoDoc.ai a welcoming space for developers to build smarter tools!

✨ *End of Code of Conduct.*
